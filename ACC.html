Adult Degree Completion Survey

Did you attend college and leave without obtaining a degree? Adult College and Completion Initiative (ACC), is here to help! But first, we want to hear from you. Take our brief survey on your post-secondary education experience. 

Take the Survey Now

As an adult who has stopped out of post-secondary education, your input is critically important to improving Post-secondary completion and graduation rates for District of Columbia residents.

Please complete the survey by September 8, 2014. 

The information you share on this survey will be kept confidential. Also, be assured that you will not be contacted as a result of your participation in this survey unless you specifically request further follow up.

If you have any questions regarding this survey or would like to request the survey in an alternate format, please contact Tiffany DeJesus.
